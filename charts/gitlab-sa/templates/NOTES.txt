ServiceAccount for GitLab was created.
To get data certificate (base64 encoded) and token (decoded) run following commands:

kubectl get secret {{ .Release.Name }}-token -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} -o=jsonpath='{.data.ca\.crt}' 

kubectl get secret {{ .Release.Name }}-token -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} -o=jsonpath='{.data.token}' | base64 -d
